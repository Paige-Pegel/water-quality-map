El Paso County Water Quality

This map visualizes the concentrations of heavy metals, flocculants, and corrosion inhibiting compounds found in groundwater samples across El Paso County in Colorado Springs. Larger circle radii correlate to higher concentrations of substances detected in tests and vice versa. It is created using the National Water Quality Monitoring Councils Database .
